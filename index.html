About Us

Welcome to Roshni Electricals, your premier destination for all your electrical needs. Established with a passion for excellence and a commitment to customer satisfaction, we have been serving our community for 50 years.

At Roshni Electricals, we pride ourselves on offering a wide range of high-quality electrical products and services to meet the diverse needs of our customers. Whether you're a homeowner, contractor, or business owner, we have the expertise and resources to help you find the perfect solutions for your projects.

Our team consists of knowledgeable professionals who are dedicated to providing expert advice and personalized assistance to ensure that you get the products and services that best suit your requirements. From lighting fixtures and electrical components to wiring and automation systems, we have everything you need to power your projects with confidence.
